Event Overview

After its three-year hiatus, West Visayas State University's Green Day is back!

With a goal to nurture a clean, safe, and productive learning environment for every Taga-West, the University Student Council prepared various activities on October 20, 2023.

Green Day 2023: Pinasubli Kagapon, sa Karon, kag sa Gihapon anchors to the theme: Sowing Sustainability and Environmental Preservation in the 21st Century.

Which provides an opportunity for environmental organizations in the University to demonstrate their knowledge, recognize their contributions, and amplify the need for climate action and justice in the twenty-first century.

See you on Friday for a greener and cleaner WVSU, fellow Taga-West!
